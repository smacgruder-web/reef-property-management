# Reef Property Design System: Aquatic Precision

Reef Property embodies a serene, coastal lifestyle through its design system, Aquatic Precision. This system evokes the tranquility of oceanfront living with soft, airy palettes and fluid interactions that prioritize clarity and calm.

## Visual Identity

The color palette draws from aquatic hues, with a primary teal (#006b5f) that conveys trust and natural harmony. Backgrounds use soft lavender-tinted whites (#faf8ff) to suggest the freshness of sea air, while surfaces layer with subtle gradients for depth without overwhelming the user. Tertiary accents in mint greens add vitality, balanced by muted secondary blues for secondary actions.

Typography leverages Manrope for headlines, providing a modern, rounded sans-serif that feels approachable yet professional. Inter serves as the body font, ensuring excellent readability across devices. Together, they create a typographic hierarchy that's clean and inviting, mirroring the simplicity of beachside architecture.

## Spatial Design

Spacing follows a consistent scale based on multiples of 4px, promoting rhythm and balance. Elements breathe with generous padding, preventing clutter and allowing content to flow naturally, much like waves gently lapping at the shore.

Elevation uses soft, diffused shadows with low opacity, creating subtle depth that feels organic rather than stark. Rounded corners (ranging from 2px to 12px) soften interfaces, reinforcing the system's fluid, nature-inspired aesthetic.

## Interaction & Motion

Motion is deliberate and smooth, with standard easing curves that feel natural and unhurried. Animations enhance usability without distraction, guiding users through flows like navigating coastal paths.

## Design Intent

Aquatic Precision aims to transport users to a state of relaxed productivity, where complex property management feels as effortless as a day by the sea. Every token supports this vision: colors that soothe, typography that communicates clearly, and spacing that provides breathing room. The system adapts gracefully across light and dark modes, ensuring the coastal calm persists in any environment.</content>
