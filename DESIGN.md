# DESIGN.md: Reef Property Management Ecosystem

## 1. Project Overview & Creative North Star
**Reef Property** is a premium, multi-role property management platform defined by the **"Aquatic Precision"** aesthetic. The design prioritizes clarity, professional integrity, and high-fidelity visual storytelling, catering to Residents, Guests, and Property Owners.

## 2. Design System: Aquatic Precision
The visual language is unified by a clean, coastal-professional theme.

### 2.1 Core Tokens
- **Primary Color:** `#14B8A6` (Teal) — Used for primary actions, branding, and status indicators.
- **Typography:** 
    - **Manrope:** Utilized for headlines and brand elements to convey modern precision.
    - **Inter:** Used for body copy and UI labels to ensure high legibility.
- **Roundness:** `ROUND_FOUR` (4px) — Soft but precise corner radii.
- **Color Mode:** Light (Default), with high-contrast Slate/Navy surfaces for dark mode elements.

## 3. User Role Architecture & Flows
The ecosystem is organized into four distinct navigation frameworks (Shells):

### 3.1 Resident App (The Tenant Experience)
*Focus: Home life, finance, and community.*
- **Key Flows:** Rent Payment, Maintenance Reporting (3-step), Amenity Booking, Community Social.

### 3.2 Guest App (The Vacation Experience)
*Focus: Discovery, premium stay, and local insights.*
- **Key Flows:** Map Discovery, 3-step Booking (Details > Dates > Summary), Guest Guide Access.

### 3.3 Owner & Manager Portal (The Investor Suite)
*Focus: ROI, Risk Mitigation, and Portfolio Health.*
- **Key Flows:** Performance Analytics, Risk Radar Monitoring, Smart Rate Optimization.

### 3.4 Technical & Support (The Infrastructure)
*Focus: System health and documentation.*
- **Key Flows:** Health Monitoring, API Integration, Legal Compliance.
