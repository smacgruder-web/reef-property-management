## 1. Executive Summary
Reef Property is a premium, high-fidelity property management ecosystem designed to serve three distinct user roles: Residents, Vacation Guests, and Property Owners/Managers. The platform prioritizes "Aquatic Precision"—a clean, professional aesthetic tailored for coastal and luxury property markets.

## 2. Problem Statement & Opportunity
*   **Problem:** Property management often feels fragmented across different apps for maintenance, payments, and guest services. Owners lack high-level analytical tools for risk and rate optimization.
*   **Opportunity:** Create a unified ecosystem that handles the entire property lifecycle—from marketing and guest booking to long-term resident management and advanced owner analytics.

## 3. Target Audience
*   **Resident:** Long-term tenants focused on ease of payment and maintenance.
*   **Vacation Guest:** Short-term travelers looking for a premium discovery and stay experience.
*   **Property Owner/Host:** Investors focused on ROI, market trends, and risk mitigation.
*   **Property Manager:** Professional admins overseeing large portfolios and building operations.

## 4. User Stories & Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Resident Experience
*   **Dashboard:** View rent status, building news, and active maintenance tickets at a glance.
*   **Payments:** Securely manage payment methods and view detailed transaction history.
*   **Maintenance:** A 3-step flow to report issues with category selection and photo uploads.
*   **Community:** Access a social feed and RSVP to building events via a community calendar.

### 4.2 Guest Experience
*   **Discovery:** Search properties via an interactive map with real-time price pins.
*   **Booking:** A seamless flow from property details to date selection and secure checkout.
*   **Stay Management:** Access a tailored Guest Guide for Wi-Fi, amenities, and local insights.
*   **Feedback:** Post-stay reviews with photo uploads and detailed rating categories.

### 4.3 Owner & Manager Experience
*   **Unified Dashboard:** Executive overview of occupancy, revenue trends, and portfolio health.
*   **Risk Radar:** Real-time monitoring of weather, travel advisories, and civil unrest with projected financial impact.
*   **Smart Rate Predictor:** AI-driven rate recommendations based on seasonality and market data.
*   **Marketing Boost:** On-demand promotional tools to push property visibility to external platforms.
*   **Financial Reporting:** Detailed payout ledgers and a secure tax/document center.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
*   **Design Consistency:** All 40+ screens adhere to the "Aquatic Precision" system (Teal #14B8A6, Manrope/Inter typography).
*   **Technical Integration:** Built for VS Code implementation using Tailwind CSS and Vite.
*   **Deployment:** Branch-based isolation for core production screens vs. kiosk demo modes.
